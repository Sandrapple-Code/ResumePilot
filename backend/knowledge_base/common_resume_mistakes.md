# Common Resume Mistakes

Avoid these frequent mistakes that flag resumes as low quality or fail ATS filters.

## List of Common Mistakes
1. **Using Passive Verbs**: Beginning bullets with words like "Responsible for", "Tasked with", "Helped", or "Worked on".
2. **Missing Metrics**: Describing responsibilities instead of achievements. Make sure to quantify outcomes (percent, hours, bytes, dollar value).
3. **Keyword Stuffing**: Listing 50 skills in a footer block without context. ATS filters often penalize list-stuffing without corresponding experience bullet points.
4. **Poor Formatting**: Using non-standard fonts, multiple colors, or multi-column grids that confuse text extractors.
5. **Overlong Descriptions**: Writing paragraphs instead of bullet points. Keep bullets to 1-2 lines max.

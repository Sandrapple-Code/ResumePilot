# HR Evaluation Checklist

This checklist summarizes what HR screeners and recruiters scan for in the first 6 seconds of looking at a resume.

## Key Scanner Highlights
- **Active Role Alignment**: Does the current or most recent job title match the target opening?
- **Core Skill Density**: Are the top 3-4 required skills mentioned in the top half of the page?
- **Impact & Scale**: Does the candidate show scale metrics (e.g. "team of 10", "managed $50K budget", "500K daily active users")?
- **Professional Stability**: Chronological dates should not contain unexplained gaps of more than 6 months.
- **Educational Verification**: Clear degree indicators and institution names.
- **Visual Cleanliness**: Sufficient white space, clear margins, and consistent section fonts.

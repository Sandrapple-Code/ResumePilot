# Resume Writing Best Practices

General standards for resume content quality, spelling, grammar, and section layout.

## Structure & Sectioning
Your resume should clearly delineate the following core sections:
1. **Contact Information**: Name, professional email, phone number, LinkedIn, and GitHub links.
2. **Professional Summary**: A 3-4 sentence paragraph highlighting your core value and years of experience.
3. **Skills / Competencies**: Grouped by category (Frontend, Backend, DevOps, Tools).
4. **Work Experience**: Reverse chronological order with company, title, dates, and bullet points.
5. **Projects**: Showcase 2-3 prominent engineering projects.
6. **Education**: Degrees, institution names, and graduation dates.

## General Proofreading
- **Spelling**: Proofread meticulously. A single typo in a programming keyword (e.g. "Pyton" instead of "Python") can flag the resume as low quality.
- **Quantification**: Target having at least 60% of experience bullet points contain numerical results.

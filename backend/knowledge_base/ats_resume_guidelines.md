# ATS Resume Guidelines

This document outlines key technical guidelines to ensure your resume is correctly parsed by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

## Technical Parser Compatibility
- **Format**: Upload resumes in PDF or DOCX formats. Avoid using image-based PDFs, as parsers cannot read scanned text.
- **Layouts**: Use standard single-column or spacious layouts. Multi-column tables, text boxes, and side bars often confuse ATS parsers, mixing text in incorrect reading orders.
- **Tables**: Do not put essential contact information or work details inside complex tables.
- **Headers & Footers**: Avoid placing contact information inside the page Header or Footer section, as some older ATS parsers ignore these zones.

## Keyword Optimization
- **Keyword Match**: Ensure core skill words (e.g. *Docker*, *TypeScript*, *Kubernetes*) are spelled exactly as they appear in the job description.
- **Density**: Do not stuff keywords. Incorporate them naturally in context bullet points.
